Welcome To Dine-out-Restaurent

Dine out brings incredible blends of various cultures to the Desert Shores community in Dhaka. The restaurant offers fine dining with a blended cultural theme of modern American cuisine. Dine out authentic dishes draw inspiration from early American cuisine with mixed European interpretations. Our restaurant is located at Mirpur Road, Dhanmondi-32, Dhaka and hours of operation are Tuesday-Saturday 11:30 a.m-10 p.m., closed Mondays.Sunday Night Dinner from 7 p.m.-11 p.m. For more information, call us directly at 702-331-5565
